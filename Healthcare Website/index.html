About Hcare Industries

Hcare Industries is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company that manufactures various Medical Disposable Products and Newborn Baby Kits. Established in 2015, the company aims to provide alternatives to traditional linen or cloth-based products for hospitals and operation theatres. Their products are known for their quality, sterility, and use of modern non-woven materials, ensuring the highest standards in medical uses. The manufacturing facility is based in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India.
